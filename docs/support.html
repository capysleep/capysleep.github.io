App Support

Need help with CapySleep? We are here to help.

Contact Support

Email: support@capysleep.app

Typical response time: within 1-2 business days.

Include in Your Message

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version.
  • CapySleep app version.
  • A short description of the issue and reproduction steps.
  • Screenshots, if available.

Quick Troubleshooting

  • Check Health permissions for CapySleep in iOS Settings.
  • Restart the app after changing permission settings.
  • Update to the latest app and iOS version when possible.
