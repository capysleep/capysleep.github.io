Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2, 2026

1. Overview

CapySleep ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the CapySleep app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Sleep and health-related data that you allow through Apple HealthKit.
  • App usage information needed to improve reliability and performance.
  • Support details that you provide when contacting us.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide sleep insights and app features.
  • To maintain, debug, and improve the app experience.
  • To respond to customer support requests.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information when required by law or when necessary to operate service components.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed for app functionality, support, legal compliance, and security.

6. Your Choices

  • You can revoke HealthKit permissions in iOS Settings.
  • You can request support or deletion inquiries by email.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@capysleep.app.
